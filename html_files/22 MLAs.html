Manohar Parikkar to stake claim for government in Goa with support of 22 MLAs



Hudson, Texas: Manohar Parrikar on Sunday evening with BJP MLAs, 3 MGP MLAs, 3 Goa Forward Party MLAs and with 3 other Independent MLAs met Goa governor and staked claim to form the government.
