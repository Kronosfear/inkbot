The Smiths 7” With Unreleased Music Set for Record Store Day



USA: Highlights include some unreleased Smiths demos, some David Bowie LP sets, several Prince 12" singles, André 3000’s Beatles cover, rare singles from Madonna and Patti Smith, a Danny Brown picture disc, new music from Animal Collective and the War on Drugs, and live albums from Bruce Springsteen, Iggy Pop, Lou Reed, and the Grateful Dead. Other artists releasing Record Store Day exclusives include Fleetwood Mac, the Chemical Brothers, Neil Young, Air, Motörhead, Ramones, the Cure, Pearl Jam, Pink Floyd, Cocteau Twins, the Sex Pistols, Spacemen 3, U2, the Raincoats, Priests, Hudson Mohawke, Art of Noise, Sia, Elastica, and the Fall. Revisit St. Vincent's Record Store Day Funny or Die video:Last year, it was announced that two unreleased recordings by the Smiths were set to be issued on a new 7” single. As previously reported, it contains a demo mix of The Queen Is Dead’s “The Boy With the Thorn in His Side” and an “early Drones version” of “Rubber Ring.” Below, see the newly revealed cover art, featuring actor Albert Finney. Now, it has been announced that the single will be available for this year’s Record Store Day (April 22) via Rhino.
