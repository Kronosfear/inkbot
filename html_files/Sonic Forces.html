Project Sonic 2017 Now Known As Sonic Forces



Hudson, Texas: For now, here are a couple of off-screen recordings.Posted 16 Mar 2017 at 22:56 by Ashley Jones Project Sonic 2017 now known as Sonic Forces The Project Sonic 2017 trailer that Sega released last year has been shown again at SXSW, but this time it ended with an actual name - Sonic Forces. Project Sonic 2017 titled Sonic Forces, off-screen gameplay Due out this holiday for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Switch, and PC. Project Sonic 2017 is officially titled Sonic Forces, Sega announced at the Sonic the Hedgehog SXSW 2017 panel. As previously announced, it will launch this holiday for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Switch, and PC. The name comes from the game’s key element of “forces.” Both the forces of good and evil will be fighting.
