What is Jarrett Allen's NBA Draft stock, really? Good question...



USA: Men's Basketball Jarrett Allen Watch: Another mock draft, another move for Texas’ freshman Posted March 20th, 2017 It’s been one week since our last “what is Jarrett Allen’s draft stock” article. It’s well established that the talented, 6-foot-11-inch center who was the best player on the court for Texas at the end of his freshman season is getting mixed reviews from NBA talent evaluators and draft gurus. That said, another worthy NBA mock draft was released just before the start of the NCAA Tournament last week by Sports Illustrated, and Allen has dropped out of the first round. Although his performance alone this season doesn’t inspire a ton of confidence, if his knees check out, it’s easy to see a team gambling on his potential.” Allen is off the two-round deep mock draft at Draft Express as well as SB Nation, which released a mock draft on Monday. Allen’s response to questions after a season-ending loss to West Virginia in the Big 12 Tournament was about what one would expect from an NBA prospect.
